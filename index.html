AfricaFeritilizer.org

To feed out people, we must feed the soil Olusegun Obasanjo, Former President of Nigeria

AfricaFertilizer.org is a global initiative to facilitate the exchange of information on soil fertility, fertilizers, and good agricultural practices in Africa. AfricaFertilizer.org is led by IFDC, in partnership with the International Fertilizer Industry Association (IFA), the African Fertilizer and Agribusiness Partnership (AFAP), the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) through its CountrySTAT program, and the African Union Commission (AUC) and its NEPAD Planning and Coordination Agency.
