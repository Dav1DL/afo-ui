NEW WEBSITE IS NOW AVAILABLE

AfricaFertilizer.org is a global initiative to facilitate the exchange of information on soil fertility, fertilizers, and good agricultural practices in Africa. AfricaFertilizer.org is led by IFDC, in partnership with the International Fertilizer Industry Association (IFA ), the African Fertilizer and Agribusiness Partnership (AFAP), the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO) through its CountrySTAT program, and the African Union Commission (AUC) and its NEPAD Planning and Coordination Agency.

AfricaFertilizer.org has launched a new website, which represents a major step forward to improve fertilizer data accessibility and dissemination at national and international levels.

The new software platform (FENIX) is built and maintained by FAO and it is currently used by several web-based applications including FAOSTAT Data Dissemination, CountrySTAT, the Agricultural Market Information System (AMIS) and several others. The new AfricaFertilizer.org application includes advanced features for browsing and analysis fertilizer data from multiple sources and promote data exchange and integration through web services.

For a limited period, the classic AfricaFertilizer.org website will be still availanle online together with the new one.
